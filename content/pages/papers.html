--- title: Call For Papers 2011 order: 3 ---

We are pleased to announce that the Silicon Slopes Conference is requesting paper submissions for talks. This is going to be big. Think hundreds of people all in the same place to learn about JavaScript and HTML5!

If you have a topic you would like to speak on at our conference, please do not hesitate to propose a talk.

Key dates

Call for Papers Open Now
Call for Papers Closes December 31, 2011
Proposal Acceptance January 15, 2012
Accepted Paper Submissions February 29, 2012
Main Conference March 29-31, 2012

Speaker Benefits

We will pay your way here (airline ticket), your motel and give you a free pass to the event!

How to Submit a Paper

Send an email with the following to: papers@utahjs.com

  1. Your name and a bit of about you (include a bit about your qualifications regarding your presentation topic)
  2. Name of your talk
  3. A really brief summary of the presentation (around 30 words)
  4. A description of what you expect to cover (around 200 words)
  5. Who your talk is likely to interest? (frontend developer, server-side developer, IT, etc...)
  6. Who are you targeting? (beginners, intermediate, advanced developers)
  7. Topic of talk (Choose one: JavaScript, HTML5)
  8. Name and information of any co-authors

Don't panic. We don't require you to have written the technology that you want to talk about. If you use something in your day to day working life and you think that other people might benefit from using it, then feel free to propose a talk on it.

Submission Stages

Propose a Paper

You do this by following the "How to Submit a Paper" above. Please submit ASAP.

Proposal Acceptance

We will email you if your paper has been accepted.

After being contacted you will need to start on your full paper.

Accepted Paper Submissions

Please send us your final papers and slides by the final date above. Email them to papers@utahjs.com

If you do not get your paper in by the final date, we reserve the right to reject your proposal and remove all speaker benefits.

Upon receiving your final papers and slides, we will call you to get the infomation we need to purchase airline tickets to fly you to Utah.

We will send you an email detailing your airline ticket information and where you will be staying.

Main Conference

You should have the information you need to fly to Utah and where you will be staying. If you do not email us at papers@utahjs.com.
